About

FI.WAV is a DJ and producer working at the intersection of amapiano, jazz, and house music. Rooted in deep grooves and rich harmonic textures, his sound bridges the warmth of acoustic instrumentation with the pulse of electronic production.


His sets are defined by a mellow, immersive atmosphere — smooth jazz melodies drifting into the deep, rolling grooves of amapiano and house. A laidback, groovy journey perfect for late-night vibes, easy mornings, and everything in between.


As the flagship artist of Vibranium Records, FI.WAV represents the label's vision of culturally rich, sonically adventurous music that connects across borders.
