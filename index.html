About

Fi.Wav is a DJ and producer working at the intersection of Amapiano, Afro House, and Jazz. Rooted in Nigerian heritage and shaped by the Amapiano and Afro House scenes — Shimza, Kabza da Small, Shakes & Les, Oscar Mbo — his sound bridges the warmth of acoustic instrumentation with the precision of electronic production.


His sets are defined by a mellow, immersive atmosphere: smooth jazz melodies drifting into the deep, rolling grooves of Amapiano and House. Music that makes you move and meditate all at once. Whether it's a rooftop at golden hour or a club at 2am, Fi.Wav reads the room and builds the journey.


As the flagship artist of Vibranium Records, Fi.Wav represents the label's vision of culturally rich, sonically adventurous music — rooted in Africa, resonant everywhere. Based between NYC, Philadelphia, and Toronto.
